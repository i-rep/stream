North Rift Radio App is an Android app which allows you to listen to live radio, read news and engage for free. It was developed as part of the station's mission to bring value to listeners. With the app, you will never miss a show or news updates as you can instantly get them from the app.

Features

~ Listen to live radio even anywhere anytime
~ Read local, regional and international news
~ Engage with other listeners at no cost
~ Follow through the current discussion topic in real time
~ Enjoy facebook and twitter without leaving the app
~ Never miss a show
