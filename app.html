How to install

After downloading the apk please follow these instructions to install

Step 1 – Go to Settings.
Step 2 – Go to Security.
Step 3 – Scroll down and check “Unknown sources” box.
Step 4 – Tap "OK" on the message box.
Step 5 – Go to your downloads and install the app.
